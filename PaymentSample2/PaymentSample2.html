payment sample 2

This payment method sample is a sample PHP implementation of a payment processor.

It does not really connecting to anything, it just directly approves every payment.

This payment sample serves purposes

1. As a sample on how to implement a payment provider for IncraShop, especially direct approved mayments like cash

2. As a test implementation to directly pay each order

Do not install this unmodified in the life environment as customers can buy products without actually paying them.
